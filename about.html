Our Story

Crafting memorable gifting experiences since 2020

Who We Are

Gift & Grate is Coimbatore's premier gifting destination, specializing in curated gift experiences for every occasion. Founded in 2020, we started with a simple mission: to transform the art of gifting into a memorable experience.

What began as a small home-based business has grown into a beloved brand trusted by thousands of customers across India. Our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and personalized service sets us apart.

Based in the cultural heart of Tamil Nadu, we blend traditional Indian gifting values with contemporary design aesthetics, creating gifts that are both meaningful and modern.

Our Mission

We believe that gifting is an art - a beautiful expression of emotions that deserves to be crafted with care. Our mission is to help you celebrate life's special moments with gifts that create lasting memories.

Every gift from Gift & Grate is carefully curated to ensure that it delights the recipient and conveys your sentiments perfectly. We source the finest materials, work with local artisans, and prioritize sustainable practices to create gifts that are as responsible as they are beautiful.

As we continue to grow, we remain committed to our founding principles: exceptional quality, personalized service, and timely delivery.

Our Values

Crafted with Love

Every gift is created with passion and attention to detail, ensuring a special experience for both the giver and receiver.

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. From the freshest flowers to the finest chocolates, we source only the best.

Eco-Friendly

We are committed to sustainable practices, using eco-friendly packaging and supporting local suppliers.
